Google Adwords (Google Ads)

Whats is google adwords

Google ads is a feature from google to show or display your ads for certain keyword that relevant with your business.

Google ads using pay per click system, where the advertiser must pay per click and pay per impression on an ad

Why you should use Google AdWords?

  • Remarketing
  • Specific Target Audience
  • minimum cost

Remarketing

Remarketing is a way to connect with people who previously interacted with your website or mobile app.

Specific Target Audience

You can narrow the range of ad locations so that the target audience will be more specific.

Minimum Cost

You can adjust your budget estimate at the lowest price, starting from $2 per day
